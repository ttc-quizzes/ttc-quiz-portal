Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning with Tushar Tuition Classes.

What is the TTC Quiz Platform?
The TTC Quiz Platform is a free online educational resource created by Tushar Tuition Classes. It offers chapter-wise MCQ quizzes for Class 6 to Class 10 students, complete with instant scores and detailed explanations.
Are these quizzes aligned with the CBSE syllabus?
Yes, all TTC quizzes are meticulously designed to align with the latest CBSE syllabus and NCERT textbooks, making them perfect for school exam preparation and revision.
Do I need to pay to take these quizzes?
No! The online quizzes provided on the TTC Quiz Platform are 100% free for all students to help democratize quality education.
How do I get my quiz results?
Upon completing a quiz, you instantly receive your score, a complete breakdown of right and wrong answers, and the option to download a comprehensive PDF report of your performance.
Where is Tushar Tuition Classes located?
Tushar Tuition Classes is a premier offline coaching institute located in Wardha, helping students achieve academic excellence across core subjects.

About TTC Quiz Platform

TTC Quiz is an educational initiative by Tushar Tuition Classes, located in Wardha. We provide meticulously crafted chapter-wise quizzes for CBSE students from Class 6 to 10. Our goal is to make learning interactive, trackable, and conceptually clear.
